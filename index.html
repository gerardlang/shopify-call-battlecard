How taxes work

Keep this answer simple. Shopify can calculate; the merchant is still responsible for compliance.

Core answer

✓Shopify calculates tax based on store settings, registrations, product categories, customer location, and shipping rules.
✓Shopify Payments processes the final total. It does not decide the tax logic.
✓Merchants usually still register, file, and remit taxes themselves unless another service handles it.

Watch-outs

  • US sales tax depends on nexus and registrations.
  • International VAT/GST depends on market, product, and local rules.
  • Duties/import taxes require proper Markets or app setup.
  • Brazil fiscal invoices / local tax flows need ERP or partner validation.
Simple line: “Shopify can help calculate and collect the right amount at checkout, but tax setup depends on where you’re registered, what you sell, and where the buyer is located.”

Plus tools to remember

Use the attract / convert / retain frame. Keep it outcome-led: more buyers, higher conversion, less operational drag.

Shopify Markets

International selling tools: local currencies, languages, domains/subfolders, market-specific pricing, duties/tax configuration, and regional catalog strategy.

Say: “Markets lets you run international storefronts from one Shopify backend instead of rebuilding your site country by country.”

Shop Pay

Accelerated checkout with saved buyer details. It reduces friction at the highest-intent moment: payment.

Say: “Shop Pay is Shopify’s fastest checkout path. The buyer can check out in a few taps instead of re-entering their information.”

Shopify Audiences

A growth tool that helps find high-intent buyers for ad platforms using Shopify’s commerce signals.

Say: “Audiences helps you improve prospecting by finding buyers who look more like people already shopping across Shopify.”

Shop Campaigns

Customer acquisition through the Shop ecosystem. Merchants set a cost-per-acquisition target and only pay when a customer converts.

Say: “Campaigns is a lower-risk acquisition lever because you’re setting the customer acquisition cost upfront.”

Shop Cash

Promotional incentive inside Shop that can give buyers extra motivation to purchase.

Say: “Shop Cash gives you a way to put an offer in front of high-intent Shop buyers without creating a blanket sitewide discount.”

Shop App

Mobile shopping surface for discovery, post-purchase tracking, re-engagement, and repeat purchase.

Say: “Shop gives the merchant a mobile channel with a built-in buyer audience, without having to build their own app from scratch.”

B2B on Shopify

Wholesale / business buying tools: company profiles, price lists, payment terms, customer-specific catalogs, and self-serve ordering.

Say: “B2B lets you serve wholesale buyers from the same platform as D2C, while still giving each company its own pricing and buying rules.”

Checkout Extensibility + Functions

Customize checkout, discounts, shipping/payment logic, and validation without fragile checkout hacks.

Say: “Plus gives you more control over checkout while keeping it upgrade-safe and performant.”

Flow

Automation for operational work: tagging customers, routing orders, fraud review, inventory alerts, VIP workflows, and internal notifications.

Say: “Flow takes repetitive manual work out of the business so teams can manage scale without adding headcount for every process.”

Customer accounts + segments

Tools for logged-in experiences, customer grouping, personalization, retention campaigns, and lifecycle reporting.

Say: “Segments help you turn customer data into actions — VIP offers, winback flows, regional targeting, or lifecycle campaigns.”
